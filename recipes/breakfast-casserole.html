Cheesy Amish Breakfast Casserole Recipe

Description

This is a hearty, Amish-inspired breakfast dish that is simple to make and serves a large crowd. The combination of classic breakfast staples like bacon, hashbrowns and eggs with gooey cheese is sure to make this a family favorite.

The total cook time is around an hour and fifteen minutes. The dish serves up to twelve people using the proportions in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon, sliced
  • 1 sweet onion, chopped
  • 4 cups frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed
  • 9 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1.5 cups small curd cottage cheese
  • 1.25 cups shredded Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir bacon and onion until bacon is evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain. Transfer bacon and onion to a large bowl. Stir in potatoes, eggs, Cheddar cheese, cottage cheese, and Swiss cheese. Pour mixture into prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake in preheated oven until eggs are set and cheese is melted, 45 to 50 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
